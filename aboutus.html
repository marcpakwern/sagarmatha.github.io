About Sagarmatha France Resturant

Sagarmatha restaurant is the first Nepalese restaurant in France, symbol of quality, expert in traditional Tibetan, Nepalese, and Indian cuisine. We receive you in a warm setting to make you discover the culture and tradition of the region « the Roof of the World ». You will have delicious traditional dishes, trademark of our restaurant. Our team will be at your service to serve you your favourite meals.
We offer 10% off on your takeaway.

Restaurant open daily, on public holidays except on Sunday evenings.

Business Hours: 12:00 - 2:30 p.m./ 7:00 p.m. to 10:30 p.m.
